The clue kinda comes from the artist name as well which is roughly "Sound Masterpiece Regiment" - it's a name used by Sharpnel and m1dy when they feel like they want to make tribute to the artists that influenced them.

In 2004, the name 音圧愚連隊 was used to release an album called 暴走歌謡大全集 ([link]; which was very roughly based around kayoukyoku/showa-era pop and early Japanese rock/punk. It is completely terrible in places. When this first came out, there was a lot of hype around it since it was omg, m1dy vs killingscum release and then it ended up a bit of a laugh for both of them as fans were wtf'd by the sound. I did talk to Sharpnel about the 音圧愚連隊 and he said anything produced under that name is not "Sharpnel sound" or "m1dy sound" but a "tribute sound" and he laughed. It's clearly not meant as a serious musical endeavour, but more of an experiment, haha.
