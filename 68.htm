@aefaradien Agreed. Twitter supporting them would be a godsend, but unlikely - I'm sure Twitter devs want it to stay simple, rather than turning into Yet Another Social Network. (And in part, I agree.) Only client I know that does it is TweetDeck, they store friendgroups in their own DB, matched up to your TD account. Obviously that means it's not portable between TD and future group-supporting clients, a pain if you have a complex setup. Implementing a format for friendgroups isn't actually too hard - the problem for SW is this: Once you have group-based columns, you don't know how many Twitter pages you have to go back in order to fill all the columns. The Twitter API isn't fast, so trawling back on every update isn't feasible. And the only way around that is caching - not a problem in desktop clients, but a very big one for SuccessWhale!
